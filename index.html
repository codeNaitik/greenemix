PRODUCTS

Nicotine Sulphate 40%

Nicotine sulphate is a solution that contains 40% nicotine sulphate and 60% water. it is a starting material for manufacturing nicotine in a purest form.

Important information

1) Nicotine sulphate is a brown colour liquid & has a pH around 5.5

2) CAS. No. : 65-30-5

3) Formula: C20 H28 N4. H2SO4

Synonyms: [-]-1-methyl-2-(3-pyridyl)pyrrolidine sulphate. The nicotine sulphate is extracted from tobacco leafs / dust.

Pure Nicotine USP/EP 99+

Pure Nicotine SUP/EP 99+ refers to pure nicotine which is an alkaloid that is extracted from tobacco plant. we produce as per the United States Pharmacopeia (USP) and the European Pharmacopoeia (EP), and has a purity level of 99% or higher.

Nicotine is an alkaloid that is use in production of various products, including nicotine replacement therapies, insecticides & e- liquid for electronic cigarette. it result in colourless viscous volatile & hygroscopic.

Pure nicotine USP/EP 99+ is typically available in various forms, including

- liquid nicotine

- nicotine salts

- nicotine base

Important information:

1) CAS. No. : 55-11-5

2) Formula : C10 H14 N2.

Safety Protocols / Standards

To ensure nicotine effectiveness it should be stored in cool & dry area away from heat, moisture & sun light.


Nicotine should be stored in freezer where it may become thick but will not freeze.


Please make sure that we should keep away from childrens & pets.


Liquid nicotine should be stored in a small plastic or glass bottles that are sealed tightly to prevent evaporation.
